Hero Banner Management

Manage hero carousel images for homepage and category pages

How It Works

Upload hero banner images for the homepage or any category page carousel. Select a target page, add a title and subtitle for the text overlay, and upload an image.

If no banners are configured for a page, the carousel will default to existing images or product images.

Advertisement Management

Create and manage ads across your store pages

How It Works

Create advertisements that display in strategic locations on your store pages. Choose a placement zone, set start/end dates, and upload an image. Ads with expired timers auto-hide.

If no ads are configured for a zone, that space remains invisible — no placeholders are shown.
